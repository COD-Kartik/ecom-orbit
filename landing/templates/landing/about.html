Our story

Built for Sellers Who Refuse to Slow Down

E-Com Orbit was born out of a simple frustration: selling across multiple platforms shouldn't mean multiplying your admin work.

Our Mission

Every day, thousands of small and growing sellers list the same product on Instagram, Facebook, Google Shopping, and Amazon — one form field at a time. When a sale happens on one channel, they scramble to update stock everywhere else before overselling. We built E-Com Orbit to make that entire process disappear into the background, so sellers can focus on what actually grows their business: their products and their customers.

Why We Started

E-Com Orbit started as a solution to a very real, very common problem faced by independent sellers scaling across social commerce and marketplaces. Rather than another isolated storefront tool, we set out to build a genuine command center — one dashboard that speaks to every channel a seller sells on, keeping listings, inventory, and orders perfectly in sync.

What We Stand For

The principles that guide how we build E-Com Orbit.

Reliability First
