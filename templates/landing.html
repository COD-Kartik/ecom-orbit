Why Sellers Are Moving to the Orbit

Selling on multiple channels generates high revenue but breeds massive operational friction. See how E-Com Orbit fixes the workflow chaos.

The Channel Chaos

  • Repetitive Listings

    Manually copying product titles, photos, descriptions, and SKU pricing onto Facebook, Instagram, and Amazon separately.

  • Inventory Mismatches

    Selling a product on Instagram and overselling it on Shopify before you have a chance to manually adjust the quantities.

  • Scattered Order Books

    Logging into 5 separate administrative panels daily to gather invoices, pack shipments, and track client delivery dates.

The Orbit Integration

  • One-Click Multi-Publishing

    Upload a product details form once, choose your target platforms, and publish instantly across connected stores.

  • Real-Time Auto Stock Sync

    Stock counts are automatically updated across all channels as soon as an order is processed on any storefront.

  • Unified Fulfillment Hub

    Gather customer details, channel tags, delivery addresses, and invoices into a single streamlined table.

Built for Omni-channel Growth

Everything you need to list products, sync inventory, fulfill orders, and track business analytics globally.

Central Product Hub

Add a product details sheet once to your central catalog, modify it instantly, and automatically map it to each sales platform.

Inventory Sync

Prevent overselling. When an order is completed on Google, E-Com Orbit instantly updates inventory on Facebook and Shopify.

Combined Order Desk

Fulfill, tag, cancel, or refund orders from various connected storefronts inside one streamlined order table.

Omni-Channel Analytics

Understand which channel is performing best. Track aggregated sales, conversion rates, and revenue trends in one chart.

Automated Synced Logs

Run automated checks on stock, status, and shipping logs. Reduce manual administrative hours by 85%.

Help & Resource Desk

Access our built-in help center, tutorials, and round-the-clock support desk directly inside your dashboard.
